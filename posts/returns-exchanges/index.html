Returns & Exchanges Policy

We want to ensure you are fully satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not satisfied, our returns and exchanges policy is designed to make the process as easy as possible.

Eligibility for Returns

We accept returns within 7 days of purchase. To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused, in its original condition, and in the original packaging.

Exchange Process

If you wish to exchange an item, please contact our customer support to given email at footer with your order number and the product you wish to exchange. The exchange process will begin as soon as we receive the item and confirm it is in resellable condition.

Refund Policy

Refunds are processed after the returned item has been inspected. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method. Please allow a few business days for the refund to appear in your account.
