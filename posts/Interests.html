Why Anime and Manga?

Let's be honest—learning anime and manga is less about cultural appreciation and more about avoiding adulthood responsibilities. Who needs real human interaction when you can obsess over fictional characters who will never disappoint you? Anime teaches you life lessons you'll never actually apply—like how to summon a dragon or defeat a god. Manga characters have more stable relationships than my own dating history. Forget saving for retirement; I'll just spend my money on collecting every Naruto figure ever made. Because waking up at 2 AM for a new episode is the responsible thing to do.

Manga Art

Manga art is a style of comic book and graphic novel illustration that originated from Japan. I am interested in becoming a mangaka to produce visually appealing manga panels with unique plots.

Drawing tools such as pencils, inking pens, erasers, and paper are essential for creating the base illustrations. Digital tools like graphic tablets and drawing software are also necessary for refining and digitizing artwork.

Reference materials like character design, panel layout, inking, shading, screentones, and storytelling are crucial for developing a well-rounded manga piece.

Animation

Animation is the process of creating the illusion of motion and change by rapidly displaying a sequence of static images that minimally differ from each other.

Drawing tools such as graphics tablets and drawing software help bring the animation to life. In addition, having a computer for rendering and editing is essential for creating animations.

Reference materials like animation books and various courses provide valuable knowledge and techniques for mastering animation skills.
